Not all management fees are charged equally, check the fine print.

  • The cleaning markup. A “$450 cleaning fee” when the cleaner is paid $300, that $150 difference is margin you never see. Ours is charged at cost and itemised.
  • The percentage trick. A headline “15%” can be charged on the total booking, including OTA and credit-card fees, so the real cost lands higher than it looks.
  • The hidden extras. Linen, tech, listing and “marketing” fees that quietly stack up. We give you a full written breakdown of every dollar, most managers won’t.

About PHH

Founded on the coast. Now managing right across Australia.

Craig Reid founded PHH in 2009 after a decade managing his own holiday properties and being repeatedly disappointed by what passed for service in the market. The original brief was simple: do the work, charge a fair fee, answer the phone.

Sixteen years on, that brief still drives the business. PHH now manages over 200 properties across New South Wales, from Sydney to the South Coast, Central Coast, Northern NSW and the Hunter, with Victoria next on the map. We've grown from our Callala Bay base to offices in Callala Bay, Culburra Beach, Berry and Sydney, with local teams of cleaners, maintenance and guest services in every region. We don't sub-contract to ghost teams. The same person who arranged your appraisal is the one you'll call in three years.

We operate as a licensed real estate agency, which means your rental income sits in a statutory trust account, audited annually, with the same protections you'd expect for a long-term residential rental. Most short-stay-only operators can't offer that.

Frequently asked

Owner questions, answered.

What commission does PHH charge?

From 18%, a single, all-inclusive commission for full-service short-stay management, with no annual listing fees, no monthly tech fees and no inflated linen costs. Cleaning is itemised at cost. The exact rate depends on property type, location and length of engagement; we'll quote you in writing during the free appraisal.

Are you really a licensed real estate agency? Why does that matter?

Yes, we hold a real estate licence and operate statutory trust accounts audited annually. For you it means your rental income is legally protected (held separately from operating funds) in a way short-stay-only operators can't offer. Most managers in this space aren't licensed, which is legal but means very different consumer protections.

How fast will I hear back if I fill in the form?

Our Australian-based team handles every owner enquiry personally, not a chatbot or an auto-reply, and we aim to be in touch promptly, usually within a business day. You'll deal with a real person from the first contact.

Where do you manage properties?

Right across New South Wales, including Sydney, the South Coast, Central Coast, Northern NSW and the Hunter Valley, with Victoria coming soon. If you're not sure whether we cover your area, just ask; we're expanding fast and may already have a local team near you.
